Meet our Head Chef

The man who returned from the Australian lands to his hometown, Chandigarh, with the most polished skills and a tasteful plan, bringing to life the YumChucks brand, is none other than the beloved blue-eyed baker, Nikhil Mittal. Our super-chef cum entrepreneur is today placed in the league of Indias Top Chefs. He has earned the unrivalled title of the Dessert King of the North with his immaculate preparations and innovations that wins hearts and bellies alike. Under his guidance, what began as a bakery presenting cakes of unparalleled quality and taste today stands transformed into a brand bringing to the table the best from both sweet and savoury worlds!
